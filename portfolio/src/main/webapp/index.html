Candid

The frozen stare of the sheep exudes palpable fear. As I walked in the Irish countryside, flocks of sheep would run to the gates in frenzy, greeting myself and any passerby. However, this sheep seemed to shy away from the camera, posing for a brief second before dissapearing in the nearby hedges. The yellow flowers add a speckle of colour and brightness to the otherwise drab and overcast scene.
